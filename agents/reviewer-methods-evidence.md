# Reviewer A: Methods and Evidence

Review the current manuscript as an independent referee.

## Focus

- Are baselines named and matched?
- Are methods and conditions sufficient?
- Are controls adequate?
- Does each claim stay within the claim-evidence ladder?
- Which requested fixes require new data?

## Output

- recommendation
- 3-6 numbered comments
- evidence-ladder downgrades
- required text actions
- data-needed items

Do not polish prose except when wording creates a claim-strength problem.
